Our Team

We pride ourselves in being a small business that brings great value. Our team consists of Co-Founders Jayden Eaton and Mark Liston.

Mission Statement

To brighten the world through the selling of computer electronics. Empowering customers with the computational power to achieve their goals.

Ethos

  • Quality: We aim to provide the world with the best computer components and accessories.
  • Transparency: We aim to be as clear as the sides of our computer cases.
  • Diversity: We recognise the benefit and necessity of different backgrounds, experiences, and opinions.
  • Innovation: We are constantly looking ahead to new advancements in computer technology.

Charity

We are committed to inspiring and enabling creativity among all generations. That is why we sponsor schools around the country, providing them with computers, free of charge.

Product Information

We are a company that sells premium computer components and accessories. From CPUs, to powerful GPUs that can enhance workflows or gaming performance and much, much more! Other components include: Motherboards, RAM, SSDs, HDDs, disk drives, power supplies, fans, water cooling units, keyboards, monitors and mouses!
